HUNT SPARROW

Instruction to play

HUNT SPARROW is an entertainment game for kids and also for those the who want to sharpe his/her aim. It is only preferable for DESKTOP/PC for now.

How to Play ?

To start the game you need to set an alias name for you. The alias name could be anything but must be with in 8 character.

After click the play button you will get the main Game floor where you can move the BOW with your MOUSE/TOUCHPAD move. There will be many passing birds with random speed. Try to aim those birds and shoot with your Mouse/Touchpad click.

You will get total 15 Arrows in your bucket. If you shoot any bird, we will restore that arrow to your bucket again.

Wish you all the best.. Lets go for Hunting..
